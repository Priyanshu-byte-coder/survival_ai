# Fire Starting Guide

## Fire Triangle
- Heat + Fuel + Oxygen = Fire
- Remove any element, fire dies

## Building Fire
1. Tinder: Dry grass, birch bark, dryer lint
2. Kindling: Small sticks, twigs
3. Fuel: Larger logs

## Methods
- Bow drill: Hardwood board, cord, socket
- Flint + steel: Sparks to tinder
- Friction: Drill method
- Lens: Magnifying glass, eyeglasses

## Fire Types
- Signal fire: Green vegetation for smoke
- Cooking: High heat, small
- Warmth: Slow burn, radiate heat
- Survival: Easy to maintain

## Safety
- Clear area around fire
- Keep water nearby
- Put out completely before leaving

Source: Boy Scouts Fire Starting Guide
