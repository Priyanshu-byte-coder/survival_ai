# Food & Foraging Guide

## Rule #1
When unsure of plant: DO NOT EAT

## Edible Basics
### Plants
- Dandelions: Leaves, root, flower
- Cattails: Root edible raw/cooked
- Plantain: Leaves cooked
- Wood sorrel: Leaves (sour taste)

### Insects
- Crickets: Cook first
- Grubs: In dead wood (cook)
- Ants: High protein
- Termites

### Fish/Animals
- Snares for small mammals
- Fish: Gut immediately, keep cold
- Birds: Use net traps

## Warning Plants
- White berries with "little people's" myth
- Milky sap plants
- Plants with umbrella-shaped flowers
- When in doubt, don't

## Calorie Needs
- ~2000 cal/day minimum
- Focus on protein/fat sources
- Nuts, seeds if available

## Bugs to Avoid
- Caterpillars with bright colors
- Bees/wasps unless certain
- Any buzzing insects without ID

Source: USDA Edible Plants Guide, SAS
