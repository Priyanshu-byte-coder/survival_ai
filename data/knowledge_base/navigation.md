# Navigation Guide

## Using the Sun
- Sunrise: East
- Noon: South (Northern Hemisphere)
- Sunset: West
- Stick method: Shadow points away from sun

## Using Stars
- North Star (Polaris): Find Big Dipper, follow pointer stars
- Southern Cross: Points south (Southern Hemisphere)

## Compass
- Keep away from metal
- Red needle points north
- Line up orienting arrow with needle

## Natural Signs
- Moss: Usually on north side of trees
- Tree growth: More branches south
- Ant hills: Often face south

## Map Reading
- Contour lines show elevation
- Blue = water, green = vegetation
- Check scale for distance

Source: USGS Navigation Handbook
