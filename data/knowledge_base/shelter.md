# Shelter Building Guide

## Priorities
- Protection from elements
- Insulation from ground cold
- Size: Just big enough to fit

## Types
### Debris Hut
- A-frame framework
- 3-4 feet wide, 7-8 feet long
- 2+ feet of debris covering
- Works in any environment

### Snow Cave
- Dig in deep snow (4+ feet)
- Minimal opening, insulate floor
- Vent hole at top
- 32°F inside even when colder

### Lean-to
- Against tree/rock
- One side protected
- Good for mild weather

## Location
- Avoid low areas (cold air sinks)
- Avoid dead trees/branches
- Check for hazards above/below
- Near water but not in flood zone

## Insulation
- Ground layer: leaves, grass, pine needles
- 6+ inches for cold weather
- Pine boughs: natural bedding

Source: SAS Survival Handbook
