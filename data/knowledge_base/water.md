# Water Sourcing Guide

## Finding Water
- Follow wildlife: birds, insects fly toward water at dusk
- Look for vegetation in dry riverbeds
- Check low areas, valleys
- Listen for running water

## Purification Methods
1. Boiling: 1 minute (3 min at high altitude)
2. Solar: UV disinfection, 6+ hours sunny
3. Filter: Use cloth, sand, charcoal layers
4. Chemical: iodine tablets, bleach (8 drops/L, wait 30 min)

## Collecting Rain
- Use tarp, poncho, any collection surface
- Channel into containers
- Cover to prevent contamination

## Dew Collection
- Tie cloths around legs, walk through grass
- Wring into container
- Most effective at dawn

## Warning Signs
- Cloudy/water with debris: purify
- No wildlife near water: may be contaminated
- Algae bloom: avoid

Source: USGS, CDC wilderness water guidelines
